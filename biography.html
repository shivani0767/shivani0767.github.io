My first introduction to Computer Science and Technology was during my middle school years when I participated in several summer workshops held at the UC San Diego Supercomputer Center. After attending the Google I/O 2015 technology conference, I founded and lead a club at my high school called Girls Who Code. During my junior year of high school, I was selected to participate in the UC San Diego Mentor Assistance Program under the guidance of Dr. Ben Cipollini (Dept. of Computer Science) where I learned about Android App Development. Currently, I am a second-year student at UC Davis majoring in Computer Science and Treasurer for the Davis Computer Science Club on campus.
